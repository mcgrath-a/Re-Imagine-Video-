Re:Imagine

Synopsis:In the mysterious afterlife, newly deceased individuals review their life's actions to validate their place for eternity. Players are subjected to harsh examination by heavenly entities (Good Guy and Bad Guy), who assess all deeds and intentions. The difficulty is demonstrating one's merit in reaching paradise or meeting a grim fate.

Group Members: Alyanna McGrath, Amylisha Mykyta, and Seolbin Hong. Part of Haptic Media Spring 2024 @ Occidental College

Let's first discuss the physical haptic elements of our game, which include the 'Big Book' and the 'stamp.' If you've seen the video—or even just the first 15 seconds—you'll notice a question posed, instructing players to write their response in the Big Book. This book is crucial at both the beginning and end of the game. Alongside it, we have a stamp marked ‘DENIED,’ which plays a vital role in the game’s final task. While I won’t spoil the ending, the book provides a tangible way for players to interact with the game, and the stamp adds a sense of permanence to the players' actions.
